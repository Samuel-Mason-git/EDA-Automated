Our Mission

Every analyst knows the pain: messy CSVs, endless null checks, duplicated columns, a dozen plots just to find what’s broken. It’s tedious. It’s time-consuming. And it gets in the way of actual thinking.

We built EDA Automated to change that. One upload, one click — and your dataset is profiled, checked, and explained, all while you refill your mug.

No scripts. No notebooks. No backend servers. Just fast, local audits.

Why We Exist

We got tired of boilerplate. Of pulling in pandas, writing the same cell for nulls, outliers, dtypes, cardinality. Of making sure no one forgot to check for VIF or collinearity before running a model.

So we asked: what if EDA wasn’t a process, but a product? What if profiling came with interpretation? What if you could hand a messy file to someone and say, “Give it 10 seconds.”

That’s the kind of frictionless audit we wanted. And now — it’s yours too.
